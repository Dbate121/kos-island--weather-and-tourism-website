Travel Tip

Best balance: May, June, and September offer the ideal combination of great weather, open businesses, and reasonable prices with fewer crowds than peak summer months.

Quick Tourist Information

  • Language: Greek (English widely spoken)
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Best areas: Kos Town, Kardamena, Tigaki
  • Local food: Gyros, Moussaka, Fresh seafood
  • Transport: Rent scooters, bikes, or cars
  • Beaches: Over 30 beautiful beaches

Must-See Attractions

  • Asklepieion: Ancient healing temple ruins
  • Kos Castle: 15th century fortress
  • Plane Tree of Hippocrates: 500-year-old tree
  • Thermal Springs: Natural hot springs at Agios Fokas
  • Bike trails: Explore the island on two wheels
  • Boat trips: Day trips to nearby islands
